# SRKR Coding Club

SRKR Coding Club is a student-led organization at SRKR Engineering College, Bhimavaram, Andhra Pradesh, dedicated to fostering a culture of coding excellence and innovation.

## About Us

We are a community of tech enthusiasts committed to empowering students through technology, learning, and collaboration. Our club provides a platform for students to enhance their coding skills, work on real-world projects, and participate in competitive programming and hackathons.

## Vision

To motivate and empower students by fostering a culture of learning, collaboration, and innovation, creating a community where they grow together and inspire others.

## Mission

Our mission is to empower students to learn and apply technology, organize projects and events that enhance skills and creativity, and build a supportive community for growth and positive impact.

## Core Values

We value learning, creativity, collaboration, integrity, and inclusivity, guiding our community to grow, innovate, and make a meaningful impact.

## Events

### HackOverFlow

Our annual 24-hour hackathon where participants collaborate to turn ideas into impactful solutions. With mentorship from industry experts, workshops, networking opportunities, and a vibrant coding environment, it's a space to learn, build, and showcase talent while creating innovative solutions to real-world problems. Features exciting prizes and internship opportunities.

### IconCoderZ

A competitive programming contest designed to test coding skills and problem-solving abilities. With beginner and expert categories, it challenges participants to think critically, code efficiently, and win exciting prizes. The contest includes algorithmic and data structure challenges across multiple difficulty levels.

### Hridayam

A social service initiative by our club members, dedicated to giving back to the community and making a positive impact through compassion and service. Our members lead community service activities and spread kindness through various social initiatives.

### Codequest

An ongoing competitive programming challenge that helps students enhance their problem-solving skills through regular coding practice.

### Edgecase

A technical event focused on testing edge case handling and debugging skills in competitive programming scenarios.
